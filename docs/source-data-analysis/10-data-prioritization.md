# 10 Data Prioritization (Refined)

## Executive Summary

Datasets are re-ranked on AI/ML usefulness, engineering feasibility, portfolio value, access ease, and industry relevance into three tiers. Tier 1 is mandatory for MVP; Tier 2 supports expansion; Tier 3 is experimental.

## Tier 1 - Core (Must Use)

| Dataset | AI/ML | Feasibility | Portfolio | Access | Relevance |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| FIRMS | High | High | High | High | High |
| VIIRS | High | High | High | High | High |
| Sentinel-2 | High | Med | High | High | High |
| Sentinel-1 SAR | High | Med | High | High | High |
| Sentinel Hub | High | High | High | High | High |
| Copernicus EMS | High | High | High | High | High |
| Global Fishing Watch | High | Med | High | High | High |
| NASA POWER | Med | High | Med | High | High |

## Tier 2 - Supporting

Landsat, NASA Earthdata, MODIS, ERA5, CelesTrak TLE, SWPC, GOES, NeoWs, Launch Library 2, OpenAQ, OISST, NOAA CDO.

## Tier 3 - Experimental

ISS stream, N2YO, SatNOGS, DONKI, OMNIWeb, SpaceX, RocketLaunch, MPC, JPL SBDB, GFW forest, synthetic telemetry, anomaly dataset, Heavens-Above.
